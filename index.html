ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Sofia Gomes, a Highschool student at Young Women’s College Preparatory Academy, fueled by a deep passion for science and research, particularly in the field of veterinary care. My goal is to become a science research veterinarian, blending my love for animals with my fascination for scientific discovery. I thrive when I'm helping others and making a difference in my community.
As a Nurturer, my work comes alive when I am able to give care and support, whether it's through volunteerism or leadership. This realization has guided my development in intuitive volunteerism and leadership, and has helped me uncover strengths in compassion, open-mindedness, and self-awareness.

These experiences have cemented my commitment to pursuing a career in veterinary science, where I can contribute to advancements that improve animal welfare and healthcare.
Looking ahead, I'm excited to continue building my skills in biology, expand my research experience, and make a meaningful impact on the world of veterinary science.
